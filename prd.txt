Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Car Deal Opportunity Detection Platform
1. Product Background
Finding underpriced vehicles in online marketplaces is currently a manual and time-consuming
process. Car dealers, resellers and individual buyers spend significant time monitoring websites
such as Divar to identify vehicles listed below market value. The purpose of this product is to
automate this process by continuously monitoring vehicle listings, comparing them against a trusted
market pricing source, identifying valuable opportunities, and notifying interested users
immediately.
2. Product Vision
Build a platform that automatically discovers attractive vehicle purchase opportunities and delivers
them to interested buyers before those opportunities disappear from the market.
3. Target Users
Primary Users: - Car Dealers - Vehicle Traders Secondary Users: - Individual Buyers
4. Core Product Concept
The platform continuously scans predefined vehicle listing sources. When a vehicle is detected, the
platform evaluates whether the listing represents a valuable purchase opportunity by comparing the
listing price against a trusted market pricing reference.
5. Product Requirements
5.1 User Management
The system must allow operators to create and manage users. For each user, the platform must
support: - Phone number (required) - First name (optional) - Last name (optional) - Source channel
(required)
5.2 Purchase Request Management
The system must allow operators to register one or more purchase requests for each user. A
purchase request represents a user's interest in a particular vehicle category.
5.3 Crawl Source Management
The platform must provide a dedicated section where operators can define crawling targets. Each
crawl target consists of: - Source website - Listing URL - Vehicle context The platform should
support future sources beyond Divar, such as Bama and other marketplaces.
5.4 Market Price Reference
For every crawled listing, the platform must obtain a market reference price from Hamrah
Mechanic. The platform dynamically generates the appropriate pricing request using vehicle
attributes such as model, year, mileage, color and body condition.
5.5 Opportunity Detection
The platform continuously scans listings from configured crawl targets. Listings are compared
against Hamrah Mechanic pricing. Only qualified opportunities are retained and distributed.
5.6 Opportunity Matching
Opportunities are matched against active purchase requests. A single opportunity may be delivered
to multiple users if relevant.
5.7 SMS Notification Flow
Whenever a new opportunity is matched to a purchase request, the platform sends an SMS
notification containing vehicle information, opportunity summary and opportunity link.
5.8 Gateway TrackingAll outbound links must pass through a platform-controlled gateway. The platform must track: -
Whether the user opened the opportunity - When the opportunity was opened - Time between SMS
delivery and engagement
6. End-to-End Product Flow
1. Create User
2. Create Purchase Request(s)
3. Define Crawl Targets
4. Crawl Listings
5. Compare against Hamrah Mechanic
6. Create Opportunities
7. Match Opportunities to Requests
8. Send SMS Notifications
9. User Clicks Tracked Link
10. Gateway Records Engagement and Redirects
7. Success Metrics
- Number of opportunities detected - Number of opportunities delivered - SMS click rate - Average
time to click - Repeat engagement rate - Performance by acquisition channel
8. Future Expansion
- Additional crawling sources - Additional pricing references - User self-service requests -
Subscription management - Advanced opportunity scoring - Automated user segmentation
